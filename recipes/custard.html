Homemade Custard

Ingredients

  • 200ml double cream
  • 700ml whole milk
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Preparation Method

  1. Put the cream and milk into a large pan and gently bring to just below boiling point.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the yolks, cornflour, sugar and vanilla.
  3. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture onto the sugar mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture onto the sugar mixture, whisking constantly.
  5. Heat gently, stirring with a wooden spoon until the custard is thickened, but before any lumps form.
  6. Eat hot or cold.
